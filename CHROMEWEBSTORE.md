## Store Listing

**Extension Name**  
Gita Wisdom New Tab

**Short Description**  
Transform your new tab into a serene sanctuary of Bhagavad Gita wisdom. Calm background themes, shlokas, translation, and audio.

**Detailed Description**  
Bring a moment of peace, mindfulness, and timeless wisdom to your busy day. "Gita Wisdom New Tab" replaces your default browser new tab page with a beautifully designed, distraction-free environment dedicated to the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita.

Each time you open a new tab, you will be presented with a hand-selected profound Sanskrit verse (shloka) from the Bhagavad Gita, accompanied by its Romanized transliteration, word-by-word vocabulary breakdowns, and complete translations in English and Hindi.

Key Features:
- Beautiful Aesthetics: 4 pre-designed calming visual backgrounds (Serene Sunrise, Divine Flute, Mystic Forest, Cosmic Gold) featuring modern glassmorphism design.
- Audio Recitations: High-quality Text-to-Speech audio reader that speaks the Sanskrit verses with authentic Devanagari pronunciation, alongside translations.
- Multi-Language support: Switch between English and Hindi translations instantly with a simple toggle.
- Word-by-Word Meanings & Explanations: Deepen your understanding of the philosophy with collapsible detailed commentary panels.
- Mindfulness Goal & Streak Tracker: Stay consistent on your spiritual journey with a daily verse counter and check-in streak tracker.
- Bookmark Favorites: Save verses that resonate with you to review anytime from the slide-out navigation panel.
- Full Index & Search: Browse or search the entire database of 25 core verses by keyword or chapter.

How to use it:
1. Click the "+" button in your browser to open a new tab.
2. Contemplate the daily shloka or listen to the pronunciation by clicking the Play button.
3. Click the menu icon in the top-right corner to customize your theme, language preference, explore bookmarks, or review all verses in the index.

Privacy & Offline-First Design:
Your privacy is our priority. This extension is built to run 100% offline. It bundles the database and assets locally and uses no external APIs, analytics, or remote tracking scripts. Your bookmarks, settings, and progress stay safely on your local device.
